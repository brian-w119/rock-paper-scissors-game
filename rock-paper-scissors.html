Rock Paper Scissors

The rules are simple. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper and paper beats rock

The user will select either Rock, Paper or Scissors. The entry will be case insensitive, i.e. "rOcK", "PAPEr", "scissors" are all valid entries.

The computetr will then randomly choose either of the three. If both the user and computer makes the same choice, and error message will received.

The winner of each round will be displayed. Five rounds will be played. GOOD LUCK! and may the best player wins.
